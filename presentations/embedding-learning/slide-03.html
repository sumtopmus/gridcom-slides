What Is an Embedding?

Classical encoding
  • High-dimensional one-hot vectors
  • Two words are always equidistant
  • No notion of similarity
Dense Embedding
  • Embedding: \( f: X \to \mathbb{R}^d \)
  • Low-dimensional vectors
  • Every dimension carries information
Geometry = Meaning
  • Distances measure semantic closeness
  • Directions encode relationships
