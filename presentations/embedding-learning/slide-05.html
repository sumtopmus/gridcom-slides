InfoNCE / NT-Xent Loss
\[ \mathcal{L} = -\log \frac{e^{\text{sim}(a,p)/\tau}}{\sum_i e^{\text{sim}(a,n_i)/\tau}} \]
  • Softmax over all pairwise similarities in the batch
  • Every other sample becomes a negative automatically
Key Insight
  • Temperature \(\tau\) controls sharpness: Small τ
    • Small \(\tau\) → sharp, peaked
    • Large \(\tau\) → soft, spread
  • Batch size matters enormously — SimCLR used 4096–8192
  • Hard negative mining is the single largest factor in quality
