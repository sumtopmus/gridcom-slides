Key Takeaways

1

Geometry = Meaning

An embedding is a learned mapping where geometric relationships encode semantic relationships. The loss function shapes the geometry.

2

Contrastive Learning Wins

InfoNCE with hard negatives is the standard. Batch size and negative mining quality matter more than model size.

3

Two-Tower for Scale

Decouple encoding from comparison. Pre-compute, index, retrieve in milliseconds. Rerank for precision.

4

The Frontier Is Multimodal

Shared vector spaces across modalities unlock cross-modal reasoning without task-specific training.

“The quality of your negative mining strategy often matters more than your model architecture.”
